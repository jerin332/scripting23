Economy

Kentville and New Minas, also known as the Kings County, is the business center of the Annapolis valley. Most modern businesses are in New Minas, while many family businesses, artisan cafes, banks, lawyer offices, investment firms and the Provincial Courthouse are situated in Kentville. Kentville is home to the Annapolis Valley Regional Hospital which opened in 1992, provides healthcare and employment opportunities to the residents of the town and the surrounding communities. The NSCC Kingstec Campus is situated in Kentville and provides world-class education.
