About

Jerin Joshy is a first-year student in the Multimedia Production program in Lethbridge College focused on the digital video and audio aspect of the course. Jerin has always been fascinated by photography. From a young age, he joined a photography club known as FDS in Bahrain where they used to go around Bahrain, capturing sunsets. That is where he sharpened most of his photographic skills specializing in landscape photography. He has worked with Bahrain Confidential, Royal Golf Club and several other commercial shoots. He has a passion for cars, coupled with his passion for photography and videography, he never misses a chance to capture a cool and unique car he spots. As a freelance photographer he has done various automotive photo and video shoots for various clients and automotive clubs.

Jerin was born and raised in Bahrain. Jerin attended in Indian School Bahrain completing his high school majoring in Commerce Marketing, before moving to Canada in 2023. He aspires to find a career in the Television Broadcasting or Film Production field. This is why he chose Lethbridge College, and the Multimedia Production program which will be a vital stepping-stone in his career.
