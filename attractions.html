Kentville

A place to live.

Attractions

Kentville is famous for maintaining its Acadian type of infrastructure throughout the years, which makes the town beautiful with all the one or two floored buildings, with all the vintage type of streetlights and textured concrete pavements, walking around Kentville feels like being in a medieval European town. U-pick farms for apples and blueberries during the summer are a well-known attraction. Restaurants like Maritime Express Cider Co and the Irish Pub is a popular spot for eating out in the town. The town consists of many beautiful parks, walking and cycling trails providing eye-catchy views and scenery of the valley.
