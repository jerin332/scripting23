Geography

Kentville is known for being the most populous town in the region, coming in at about 27,000 as of 2021. The land is well known for its fertile land for cultivation mainly for apples and different kinds of berries. Kentville is well known for its amazing summers and pleasant winters, which has attracted many retired households from around the country to settle in Kentville
